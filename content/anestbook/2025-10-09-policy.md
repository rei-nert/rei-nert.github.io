# Privacy Policy for Anesthesia Handbook

*Effective Date: October, 7th, 2025*

*Anesthesia Handbook* ("we", "us", or "our") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our mobile application.

## 1. No Data Collection

We do not collect, store, or share any personal data from users. The app is designed to function entirely offline, and all calculations and inputs are processed locally on your device.

## 2. No Internet Access

The app does not require or use internet access. It does not connect to any servers, third-party services, or analytics platforms.

## 3. No Advertisements

There are no ads in the app. You will not see any third-party advertising or promotional content.

## 4. No Third-Party Integrations

The app does not use any third-party libraries or SDKs that collect data or send information outside the device.

## 5. Local Data Storage (If Applicable)

Any data you input into the app (e.g., for calculations) is stored locally on your device (if at all) and remains under your control. You may delete this data at any time by clearing the app’s storage or uninstalling the app.

## 6. Children’s Privacy

As the app does not collect or transmit any personal data, it is compliant with privacy regulations regarding children's data, including COPPA and GDPR-K.

## 7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected within the app or through app store updates. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
